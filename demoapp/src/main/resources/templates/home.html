What Is ROOCA?
Capgemini proprietary Root Cause Nanobot focused on Problem Management that Uses NLP (Natural Language Processing) technique to identify nearest matching solution for an incident from the KEDB (Known-error Database), displaying an existing RCA and helping you get to the root cause of the problem faster while also displaying a ready solution for a similar issue which has occurred in the past
What is FMA Process?
Failure modes analysis (FMA) is a step-by-step approach for identifying all possible failures in a service. Failure modes means the ways, or modes, in which something might fail. Failures are prioritized according to how serious their consequences are, how frequently they occur and how easily they can be detected. The purpose of the FMA is to take actions to eliminate or reduce failures, starting with the highest-priority ones.
What is RPN?
The Risk Priority Number (RPN), is a numeric assessment of risk assigned to each failure that quantify likelihood of occurrence, likelihood of detection, and severity of impact.
What is SIP?
Service Improvement Plan (SIP) refers to the steps that have to be taken to permanently fix a failure.
